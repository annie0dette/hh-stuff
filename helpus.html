Get Involved

First Grace United Methodist Church members, students through our service learning partnerships, and members of the wider community all provide vital services and support to Hagar's House.

Frequent volunteer needs are for child care, children's programs, bicycle maintenance & repair, house maintenance and repair, tutoring or homework help for both adults and children, yard work & gardening, cleaning, fundraising (organizing a fundraiser of your own or staffing/helping with a fundraiser organized by the house of one of our partners), and organizing a drive for currently needed items (please contact our donations coordinator to learn what those needs are). We also welcome volunteers who offer one-time or recurring programs & services for individuals or groups of adult residents.

Should you be interested in working with us please send an email to Carolyn Mayes at hagarshousevolunteers@gmail.com with a brief message explaining
  • your interest in Hagar's House
  • how you would like to be involved
  • how to get in contact with you
and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.

Because Hagar's House is a home first and foremost, we are not usually able to accommodate large groups of volunteers.

For more information please call (504) 210-5064 or email HagarsHouse@gmail.com
